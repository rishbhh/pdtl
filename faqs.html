How much does it cost?
Class 1 and 2 – Ranges from £1,944 inclusive of VAT NOTE: this is based on an average, and will change depending on how many hours training are required – This price includes 20 hours on road training. Also based on first time pass.
FREE assessment is included to gauge how many hours are required
How many hours training will I require?
  • Practical – minimum 20 hours – even if you have some prior experience in smaller class vehicles
  • Theory – You should expect to do 10-20 hours of theory training prior to your test, which is completed online
How long does the training take?

Class 1 and 2 – End to end – can take from a minimum of 7 weeks, and typically an average of 16 weeks

Do you offer Online CPC?

Yes - You must do 35 hours of periodic training every 5 years to keep your Driver Certificate of Professional Competence ( CPC ) to drive a lorry, bus or coach. We offer online courses where you can top up your professional competence training. See our online CPC training schedule for dates.

What is the availability of medicals?
  • We have nationwide availability through our medical partners and can typically get you a medical within 2 weeks
What is the availability of tests?
  • Theory tests – on average 6 weeks
  • Practical – 12 weeks plus on average
