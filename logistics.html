Logistics

With over 30 Years Of Experience In The Logistics and Supply Chain Solutions, We work closely with our customers to deliver tailored logistical and warehousing support.

With more than 100 drivers on the road each day, backed up by the latest transport and logistics technologies and vehicles, PDTL’s team of transport professionals ensures that each of its nationwide customers receive a tailored logistical solution that helps remove client concerns regarding their delivery warehousing and distribution requirements.

The PDTL headquarters is based in the North West with very close access to Junction 21 M62 and is well placed for both national and international travel. With close links to the UK’s major road networks, our headquarters in Rochdale is the ideal location for businesses throughout the UK.

As specialists in the logistics and warehousing industry, we work closely with our partners to ensure our strategies are aligned to provide our customer base the solutions that fit their business needs. This includes maximum efficiency with our warehousing partners for goods to be re-stocked and distributed.

Weapons & Ammunition Transportation

Having been a partner of the MOD since the early 1980’s, we are well placed to meet our customers movement requirements for Weapons, Ammunition and Explosives across the UK and beyond into Europe. Our workforce of SC level security cleared drivers and our bespoke assets including EXIII tractors and Ekeri & EXII trailers make us an obvious choice for your specific requirements. Using up to date security methods and hardware, we are able to transport these goods with speed, efficiency and integrity keeping your reputation at the top – where it belongs.

Contract Logistics

If your need is for a fully managed fleet operating out of one site or multiple locations you can rest assured that this is an area we have a great deal of experience in. Offering bespoke solutions to our customers to give them the exact delivery capability and efficiency they require, is easily achievable by understanding their needs and fitting the best solution to them.
