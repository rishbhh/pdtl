Logistics

With over 30 Years Of Experience In Logistics and Supply Chain Solutions, We work closely with our customers to deliver tailored logistical & warehousing support.

With more than 100 drivers on the road each day, backed up by the latest transport and logistics technologies and vehicles, PDTL’s team of transport professionals ensures that each of its nationwide customers receive a tailored logistical solution that helps remove client concerns regarding their delivery warehousing and distribution requirements.

The PDTL headquarters is based in the North West with very close access to Junction 21 M62 and is well placed for both national and international travel. With close links to the UK’s major road networks, our headquarters in Rochdale is the ideal location for businesses throughout the UK.

As specialists in the logistics and warehousing industry, we work closely with our partners to ensure our strategies are aligned to provide our customer base the solutions that fit their business needs. This includes maximum efficiency with our warehousing partners for goods to be re-stocked and distributed.
