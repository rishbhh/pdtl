Logistics

With nearly 20 years of experience in the automotive component supply chain, we are constantly called upon to apply our expertise to deliver to a broad range of ‘Just in time’ industries and those requiring trusted and timely logistical support.

With more than 100 drivers on the road each day, backed up by the latest transport and logistics technologies and vehicles, Barnes Logistics’ team of transport professionals ensures that each of its nationwide customers receive a tailored logistical solution that helps remove client concerns regarding their delivery and distribution requirements.

The Barnes Group headquarters is well placed for both intra- and international travel. With close links to the UK’s major road networks, our headquarters in Rochdale is the ideal location for businesses throughout the UK.

As specialists in Just In Time logistics (JIT), we ensure our strategies are precisely timed for maximum efficiency with goods re-stocked and distributed before they are sold out. Our warehouse operatives keep our warehouses running smoothly and ensure we are in line with our own efficient logistics targets.
